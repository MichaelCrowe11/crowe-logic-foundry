# Non-Dilutive Grant Targets — Crowe Mycology LLC / Crowe Logic, Inc.
Date: 2026-05-24
Source: research pass against official .gov sources. Ranked strongest-fit first.
Entity notes: Crowe Logic, Inc. (DE C-corp, via Stripe Atlas) for SBIR/tech; Southwest
Mushrooms / Crowe Mycology LLC (AZ producer) for the farm/producer grants.

## TIER 1 — apply first

### 1. USDA SBIR/STTR (NIFA) Phase I — BEST FIT
- Funds R&D by small businesses on ag/food problems. Topic fit: "Plant Production &
  Protection," "Small and Mid-Size Farms," "Rural & Community Development." AI cultivation
  OS for specialty-crop production maps cleanly.
- Award: Phase I up to **$100,000** (~8 mo); Phase II up to **$600k** ($650k w/ TABA). No match.
- Eligibility: US for-profit small biz, <=500 employees, majority US-owned, PI primarily
  employed by company at award. Crowe Logic, Inc. qualifies cleanly.
- 2026 window: FY26 Phase I deadline NOT VERIFIED (NIFA had not posted as of research).
  Register on Grants.gov for the NOFO alert.
- https://www.nifa.usda.gov/grants/programs/sbir-sttr
- Pair with AZ FAST (#8) to subsidize proposal prep.

### 2. USDA Value-Added Producer Grant (VAPG)
- Adds value to raw commodity: processing, branded value-added mushroom products,
  marketing, working capital. 195K channel + branded line = strong narrative.
- Award: Planning up to **$50k**; Working Capital up to **$200k**. Requires **1:1 match**.
- Eligibility: agricultural producer. Southwest Mushrooms qualifies. Match is the hurdle.
- 2026 window (VERIFIED): **Feb 17 – Apr 22, 2026, 1:00pm ET**. Confirm w/ AZ state RD office;
  may have just closed — recurs annually.
- https://www.rd.usda.gov/programs-services/business-programs/value-added-producer-grants

### 3. Specialty Crop Block Grant (SCBGP) — Arizona / AZDA
- USDA AMS, administered by **AZDA** (existing relationship = advantage). Mushrooms ARE an
  eligible specialty crop. Angles: research, marketing, education (channel), food-safety.
- Award: varies by AZ cycle (commonly tens of thousands, up to 2yr).
- CAVEAT: cannot fund projects that solely benefit one private entity. Structure as a
  multi-beneficiary project or partner with an association/university.
- 2026 window (VERIFIED): AZDA deadline **Mar 6, 2026** (likely passed; recurs annually).
  Contacts: Lisa James (602) 542-3262; Ashley Estes (602) 316-6775.
- https://agriculture.az.gov/about-us/divisions/agriculture-food-systems-support/specialty-crop-block-grant-program-scbgp

## TIER 2 — good fits, conditional

### 4. Western SARE Farmer/Rancher Grant
- On-farm sustainability research + outreach. Substrate/yield/data study w/ channel
  dissemination = natural fit. Award up to **$35,000**, 1-3yr.
- Producer lead + technical advisor. Southwest Mushrooms qualifies.
- Window: prior cycle Nov 20, 2025; next NOT VERIFIED (recurs each fall).
- https://western.sare.org/grants/farmer-rancher/

### 5. FMPP / LFPP (USDA AMS, under LAMP)
- Direct-to-consumer marketing (FMPP) or local food enterprise dev (LFPP). 25% cost share.
- 2026 window (VERIFIED): due **June 5, 2026, 11:59pm ET** via Grants.gov.
  Contact: FMLFPPgrants@usda.gov.
- https://www.ams.usda.gov/services/grants/lamp

- https://www.azcommerce.com/programs/sbir-sttr-resource-center/
